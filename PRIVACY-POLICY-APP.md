# Tilt Desktop Application Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2025  
**Last Updated**: January 1, 2025

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Tilt ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Tilt desktop application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO Collect

**Task Instructions and Screenshots (Limited)**
- We collect anonymized task instructions with all personally identifiable information removed
- We collect screenshots taken during automation sessions
- This data is used exclusively for improving our machine learning models through reinforcement learning

### Information We DO NOT Collect

**API Keys and Authentication**
- Your Claude API key remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers
- We do not store or have access to your API keys

**Personal Data**
- We do not collect, store, or process any personal information
- We do not collect user names, email addresses, or contact information
- We do not collect device identifiers or IP addresses

**Application Data**
- Since Tilt is a desktop application, all your data remains on your local device
- We do not store your tasks, results, or any application data on our servers
- We do not track your usage patterns or behaviors

## How We Use Collected Information

**Model Improvement Only**
- Anonymous task instructions and screenshots are used solely for training and improving our AI models
- This data helps us enhance the accuracy and effectiveness of computer automation
- No personal information is included in this training data

**Data Processing**
- All collected data is stripped of identifying information before use
- Data is processed using reinforcement learning techniques
- Processed data is used to improve future versions of the software

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We Do Not Share Your Data**
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
- We do not share data for advertising or marketing purposes
- We do not disclose data to partners or affiliates

**Legal Requirements**
- We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights
- Such disclosures would be limited to the minimum necessary to comply with legal obligations

## Data Security

**Local Data Protection**
- Your data remains on your local device and is protected by your device's security measures
- We recommend using standard security practices for your device

**Server Security**
- Limited data transmitted to our servers is encrypted in transit
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we process

## Data Retention

**Limited Collection**
- We only retain anonymized task instructions and screenshots for model training purposes
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for model improvement
- Personal information is not retained as it is not collected

## Your Rights and Choices

**Desktop Application Control**
- You maintain full control over your data as it remains on your device
- You can uninstall the application at any time
- You can modify or delete your local data at any time

**Model Training Data**
- Anonymous data used for model training cannot be attributed back to individual users
- If you prefer not to contribute to model improvement, please contact us

## Children's Privacy

The Tilt application is not designed for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Third-Party Services

**Anthropic Claude API**
- The App uses Anthropic's Claude API for AI functionality
- Your API key is used directly with Anthropic's service
- Please review Anthropic's privacy policy for their data handling practices

**No Other Third Parties**
- We do not integrate with other third-party services that collect your data
- External websites or applications accessed through automation are not covered by this policy

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Providing notice within the application
- Posting updates on our website
